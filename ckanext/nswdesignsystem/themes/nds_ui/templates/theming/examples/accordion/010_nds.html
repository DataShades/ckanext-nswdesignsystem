The theme adds toggle_all parameter to accordion_wrapper to perform the action to all accordions, reducing time and clicks to reach the required information.

{%- call ui.util.call(ui.accordion_wrapper, toggle_all=true) -%} {{ ui.accordion(lipsum(1), title="Accordion 1") }} {{ ui.accordion(lipsum(1), title="Accordion 2") }} {%- endcall %}
